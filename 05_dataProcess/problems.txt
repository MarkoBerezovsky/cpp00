

European temperature data files can be downloaded from 
http://eca.knmi.nl/countries/country_info.php?coun_id=cz
The data are in  the text format and  relatively easy to manipulate.

Write a program which finds in the temperature data from Prague Klementinum (years 1775-2005) :

A. The hottest day.
B. The hottest week. Suppose that any 7 consecutive days form a week.
C. The hottest month.
D. The hottest year.
E. The hottest decade. Suppose that any 10 consecutive years form a decade.


A week, mont etc, is hotter than another week, month, etc, if the average temperature of the first one is 
higher than the average temperature of the second one.
